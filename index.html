About Me

"Buy the ticket, take the ride"-Hunter S. Thompson

I am a huge fan of music, music festivals and live shows. I've fronted a number of bands as a blues/jazz/soul vocalist, but since moving to Colorado in 2011, I've taken a break from the music scene and focused on writing my own at home. I occasionally find time to jam with other musicians and it makes my soul happy. I currently work as an Educational admin/project manager with an incredible, national association for massage therapy. I work really closely with our IS department and am so looking forward to becoming a web developer and moving into their department.
Here's to a successful 6-month program and an amazing new career!
