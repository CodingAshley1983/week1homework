About Me

"Buy the ticket, take the ride"-Hunter S. Thompson

I am a huge fan of music, music festivals and live shows. I've fronted a number of bands as a blues/jazz/soul vocalist. I play guitar, piano and ukulele, and this past summer I added "cigar-box" guitar to my instrumental repetoire when I built an electric guitar out of an 800 page book. I can't wait to make the next one!

I'm originally from Upstate NY (nowhere near NYC!) and moved to Denver in 2011. I've taken an unintentional hiatus from the music scene and focused on writing my own at home. I occasionally find time to jam with other musicians and it makes my soul happy. I currently work as an Educational admin/project manager with an incredible, national association for massage therapy.

I work really closely with our IS department and am so looking forward to becoming a web developer and moving into their department.
Here's to a successful 6-month program and an amazing new career!
